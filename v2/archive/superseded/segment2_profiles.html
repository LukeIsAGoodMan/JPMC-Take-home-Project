Full V2 Two-Layer Architecture

Population (199,523) ├── Seniors (~14%) → retirement products ├── Youth/Dependents (~28%) → family-indirect └── Working-Age (~58%) ├── Active Workforce (76%) → primary targeting └── Non-Employed (24%) → household-indirect

Status: Exploratory. The second-pass found 2 stable sub-segments (employed vs non-employed). Finer splits were unstable — every k≥3 produced micro-clusters in k-evaluation. The employed/non-employed split is meaningful for marketing but does not provide the occupation-level granularity originally hypothesized. The classifier score serves as the continuous sub-prioritization dimension within the Active Workforce sub-segment.
